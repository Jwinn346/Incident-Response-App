Incident Response Capture Guide

⏰ Record the exact time the call was received.

📞 Identify the caller (Signaller, emergency service, train driver, public).

📍 Enter the nearest known location, landmark, or track mileage.

⌛ Is this happening now or did it happen earlier? If past, is there any ongoing risk?

📍 Where exactly is the trespasser? On the track, embankment, or nearby?

➡️ Which way is the trespasser moving? Towards or away from the railway?

🧐 Describe their appearance. Clothing, age, number of people?

👶 Are they a child or young person? Approximate age?

🚨 Have emergency actions been taken (e.g., Signaller informed, train cautions imposed)?

🚔 Who is attending? List all emergency services involved.
