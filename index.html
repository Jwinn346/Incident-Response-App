Incident Response Capture Guide

👀 Who has reported this? Are they on-site or relaying second-hand information?
👀 If it's a past incident, is there any ongoing risk or concern?
👀 Has the caller lost sight of them? Are they still visible? If they left, when and in which direction?
❓ Describe the situation in full. Where is this taking place? What direction is the person moving in? What does the person look like?
👀 Visualization prompt: If I was standing there, what would I see?
🚨 Is an emergency call to the Signaller/ECO required? ⚠️ If a danger exists, has a caution been imposed?
👀 Double-check the response plan: "Before I end the call, have I got everything?"
Ask “What else?” before ending the call.
